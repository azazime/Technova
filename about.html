About Technova

Pioneering Eco-Friendly Cleaning Solutions

Our Mission

Technova is a pioneering eco-friendly cleaning company dedicated to providing top-notch cleaning services while minimizing our environmental impact. Our mission is to create cleaner, healthier spaces for our clients without compromising the health of our planet.

Founded on the principle that cleanliness and sustainability can go hand in hand, we have developed innovative cleaning techniques and products that are both effective and environmentally responsible.

At Technova, we believe that every cleaning job is an opportunity to make a positive impact on our environment. We're committed to continually improving our methods and educating our clients about the benefits of eco-friendly cleaning.

Our Goals

  • Reduce water waste in cleaning processes
  • Eliminate the use of harmful chemicals
  • Promote the use of biodegradable cleaning products
  • Educate clients on sustainable cleaning practices
  • Minimize our carbon footprint in all operations

Meet Our CEO

Kalu Mark

Kalu Mark is the visionary founder and CEO of Technova. With over 15 years of experience in the cleaning industry, Kalu recognized the need for more sustainable cleaning solutions and founded Technova to address this gap in the market.

Under Kalu's leadership, Technova has grown from a small local operation to a respected name in eco-friendly cleaning services. Kalu's passion for environmental conservation drives Technova's commitment to innovation and sustainability in all aspects of our business.
